Nyx

Nyx emerges from primordial darkness, cloaked in a veil of stars and silence. As the personification of Night, she drapes the world in her gentle shadow, where every whisper and dream is sacred. Even the gods honor her with hushed reverence—within her midnight realm, all beginnings and endings entwine. In the quiet awe of her presence, we find both solace and mystery, guided by the soft glow of stars she scatters across the sky.
